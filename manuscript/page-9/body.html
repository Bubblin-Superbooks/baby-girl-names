The challenge was always finding the right keyword. Sonica and I love both Caucasian and Asian names but we did not want our daughter's name to be influenced by race, religion or what's popular in the US today.

Her name had to be simple, but also reflect multi-culturalism. It had to be short and easy to spell. And with so many flexible constraints, it was imperative that we scanned through as many names as possible.

And so was born this book. A collection of 70,000 alphabetically sorted girl names sourced and compiled from the names of last 100 years in America. With this book in your hands, you cannot miss a good name for your daughter. Contributions are welcome, if you do!

Finding a great name for your daughter is a big responsibility. I hope you will find this book as useful as we did to find a name for our baby.
