The challenge was always finding the right keyword. And its meaning or usage all around the world. Sonica and I love both Caucasian and Asian names but we did not want our daughter's name to be influenced by race, religion or what's popular in the US today.

Her name had to be simple, but also reflect multi-culturalism. It had to be short and easy to spell. And with so many flexible constraints, it was imperative that we scanned through as many names as possible.

And so was born this book.

A collection of 70,000 alphabetically sorted girl names sourced and compiled from the names used in last 100 years of America. With this book in your hands, you cannot miss a good name for your daughter. Contributions are welcome, if you do!

Finding a great name for your daughter is a big responsibility after all. We landed on Zuvy for our child and I hope you will find a name for your baby too.
