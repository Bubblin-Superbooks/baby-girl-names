Introduction

Hey there!

Welcome to the universal book of baby girl names.

First off, congratulations on the news of your baby. This must be so exciting! I know that feeling because Sonica and I are going through our first pregnancy right now, as we create this book.

A collection of 70,000 alphabetically sorted girl names sourced from all over the world. With this book in your hands, you cannot miss a good name for your daughter. Well, if we have missed one, feel free to contribute with pull requests!

The book is open source.

Finding a great name for your daughter can be challenging. And the challenge is always in finding the right keywords. Its meaning and usage all around the world in as many languages as possible.
