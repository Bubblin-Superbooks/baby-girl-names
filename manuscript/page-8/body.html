Introduction

Welcome new parents. Congratulations for the news of your baby girl. This must be so exciting for you!

I know the feeling because Sonica, my wife, and I are going through our first pregnancy ourselves. And while we are at it, we are always looking for a cool and unique name for our daughter. Have a suggestion to make?

One thing that I noted about our search for the name is that we would often repeat our searches with a keyword, a multiple times a day, over weeks, in a hope to incorporate a special meaning that we liked, or an honor or a social thought. Or we would go in the direction of baking the family tree or a cultural tradition into the name. It was tiring.

The challenge was always finding the right keyword.
